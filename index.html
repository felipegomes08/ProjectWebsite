About Us

It is a typical Italian house, with a special atmosphere, which will take you to a legitimate canteen located in a small village in Italy.
In it we offer the best of gastronomy, with handmade pasta and a cellar with more than 100 of the best wine labels in the world.
Ruotolo's Posto is a place with a big table, loud conversation and rosy cheeks. Come live moments of joy at the table with your family, your friends and your loves.
